I'm building Bezier — an AI-native tool for makers.

The idea: dissolve the handoffs between PM, design, engineering, and QA, so one person can move from spec → design → working prototype → QA in a single, continuous loop. I'm a product manager by trade, building it mostly solo (with a lot of help from AI).

I can ship the product. What I can't do well is the craft side of the brand — and Bezier deserves an identity that looks as considered as the product is trying to be.

So I'm looking for a freelance designer to own two things:

• Logo / brand identity — there's a clear starting direction (monochrome, pen-tool-derived) to refine, or to reinvent if you see something better.
• Landing page — design the page that introduces Bezier to the world (and, if you build too, even better).

You might be a great fit if:

• You do both brand identity and web/landing design
• You have taste for restraint — clean, editorial, confident; the opposite of templated
• Bonus: you've designed for dev tools, product, or AI companies

This is a paid freelance engagement — remote, async-friendly, project-based to start, with room for ongoing work as Bezier grows.

Interested? Comment with a link to your portfolio (or DM me). I'll reply to everyone who shares their work.

Excited to see what you'd make.
